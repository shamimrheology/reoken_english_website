Transcriptomics Analysis Softwares

Our Transcriptomics division has developed sophisticated software tools for the analysis of gene expression data, enabling researchers to visualize and interpret complex transcriptomic landscapes.

We are embarking on an exciting journey to develop the next generation of cutting-edge software and tools for advanced Bioinformatics studies. Our mission is to empower students and researchers by simplifying complex Bioinformatics analyses, eliminating the need for deep expertise in programming or hardware. We invite visionary developers and potential collaborators to join us in this transformative effort. Together, we can revolutionize Bioinformatics by creating intuitive, high-performance tools that bridge the gap between biological research and computational innovation.

Please join us and be a part of shaping the future of scientific discovery!

We developed softwares to generate simple network analysis from complex data (RNASeq).

Our user-friendly New Software allows you to create Signaling Pathways effortlessly and with precision from RNASeq data. Designed for ease of use, even beginners can generate high-quality pathways by managing complex datasets with simplicity. This software is an invaluable tool, not only for students but also for advanced researchers looking to streamline their analysis.

We developed user friendly software to generate publication quality plots using RNASeq data.
