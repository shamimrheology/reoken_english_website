Brain Fatigue Research

Our Brain Fatigue research aims to understand the underlying biological mechanisms that cause cognitive fatigue. By studying neural pathways and metabolic processes, we seek to develop effective interventions and technologies to reduce fatigue and enhance cognitive performance.

BOOCS: A Breakthrough in Brain Health

What is BOOCS?
BOOCS stands for:
B: Brain
O: Oriented
O: Oneself/Obesity
C: Control/Care
S: System
Developed by Dr. Takehiko Fujino, Emeritus Professor of Kyushu University, BOOCS is a revolutionary method designed to combat Brain Fatigue and restore brain health. Grounded in over 15 years of research, BOOCS integrates science and care to rejuvenate the brain, relieve stress, and prevent neurodegenerative conditions like Alzheimer’s Disease.
How BOOCS Works ?
BOOCS approaches brain health holistically by focusing on: Relieving Brain Fatigue: Enhancing sleep, appetite, and energy levels. Restoring Brain Function: Supporting memory and cognition through the regeneration of damaged brain cells. Reducing Stress: Encouraging patients to enjoy their favorite activities rather than adhere to rigid self-control, which stimulates the senses and promotes relaxation. Stimulating Plasmalogen Production: A key compound for maintaining brain health and slowing neurodegeneration.
The Importance of BOOCS
Brain Fatigue, often caused by chronic stress, is a precursor to many health conditions, including: High cholesterol, hypertension, and diabetes. Depression, poor memory, and irritability. Neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer’s. BOOCS provides a clear, science-backed pathway to reverse Brain Fatigue, making it easier for individuals to lead healthier, more balanced lives.
Who Should Consider BOOCS?
BOOCS is ideal for anyone aiming to: Reduce stress and improve overall brain function. Prevent Alzheimer’s and other metabolic syndromes. Manage weight and improve eating habits. BOOCS is effective for people of all ages, from adolescents to the elderly, offering a comprehensive method to prevent and treat Brain Fatigue.
