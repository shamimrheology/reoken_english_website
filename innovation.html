Our Innovations

IRFF Genome Institute is at the forefront of developing innovative tools that push the boundaries of scientific research. Explore our latest tools designed to facilitate advanced studies in genomics, lipidomics, and behavioral neuroscience.

Our Innovative Tools

Behavioral Dynamics Analyzer: Precision Tracking and Quantification System

We have developed an advanced multi-point tracking and analysis system designed to capture a wide range of behavioral data for organisms like C. elegans and mice. Unlike existing systems, our innovative solution can detect and quantify real-world shape changes with unprecedented precision. This system enables the detection of subtle behavioral parameters, allowing for precise differentiation of behavioral changes and quantification of treatment effects. By leveraging sophisticated algorithms and visualization tools, our system plots intricate shape dynamics and behavioral metrics, offering researchers and companies a powerful way to analyze the effects of interventions and treatments. This cutting-edge tool is ideal for laboratories, research institutes, and organizations seeking to push the boundaries of behavioral science and drug development.

Automatic Genomic Analyzer (Transcriptome and Mutation)

Revolutionizing Genomic Analysis with Next-Generation Tools. We are at the forefront of developing cutting-edge platforms for next-generation genomic sequencing analysis, offering unparalleled capabilities in transcriptome profiling, mutation and variant detection, and signaling pathway analysis. Our solutions combine intuitive, user-friendly interfaces with powerful data processing, making advanced genomic analysis accessible to researchers, students, and institutions without requiring programming expertise. Our software enables seamless analysis of genomic data, including FASTQ formats, to generate high-quality, publication-ready results with minimal effort. By integrating state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, our platforms deliver precise predictions to identify critical gene targets and pathways, driving advancements in disease prognosis and therapeutic strategies.
